charming hotel in Normandy

In the valley of the river Seine, very near Les Andelys and its ruins of the ancient castle protecting against the English invaders and close to Giverny and the Monet gardens and painting atelier, Chateau Corneille(科尔奈城堡酒店), an XVIIIth century old mansion, is nestled in its beautiful 1.5-hectare park, surrounded with meadows and forests. It is said that this castle was built on the ruins of an ancient post relay where Pierre Corneille the famous writer used to stay overnight during his frequent trips to Paris to the court of Louis the XIV.

Between Paris and the sea, close to Rouen, Deauville, Cabourg, Trouville and Honfleur, as well as the Normandy coast and the 1944 D-Day landings beaches, this charming 19-room hotel offers its guests peace and tranquility in a delightful green setting. Château Corneille welcomes you both for a slow-life weekend and for your intimate events: weddings, family reunions, dinners with friends, any event so enjoyable in a place at the crossroads of nature and heritage in the heart of departement Eure.

Its restaurant La Closerie, located in the former stables, provides you with an authentic and friendly atmosphere around its large fireplace. On sunny days, the terrace offers a view extending to the horizon and invites you to quiet meals in the colorful of Normandy.
