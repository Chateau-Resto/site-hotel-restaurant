Animal Friendly

Domestic animals (<15kg) are welcome to stay with you in our hotel. Please prepare bedding for them (cage, basket, mat...).

General Information

Check-in time is 3.00pm. Check-out time is 11.00am.
Our restaurant La Closerie opens 7 days a week, it closes on Sunday evening, Monday and Saturday lunch time.
For your well being as well as the others, the hotel and the restaurant is entirely NO SMOKING.

A parking area is available on the premises and can be accessed freely between 8:00 AM and 10:00 PM (3:00 PM on Sundays). If you plan to arrive late, please contact the reception between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM for the gate access code.
